About Us

Indiana Soccer Referees Association is responsible for assigning referees to U.S.S.F.-sanctioned club matches in the greater-Indianapolis area. This online system is to assign referees to games. Each soccer game may have one to four officials assigned, depending on the level. A referee only for low-level recreational games, a referee and two assistant referees for most games, and an additional “fourth official” for certain higher- level games. This custom application helps track the assignments and status of each of the referees.
